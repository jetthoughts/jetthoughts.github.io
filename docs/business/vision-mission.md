# JetThoughts - Vision, Mission, Positioning

**Owner**: Paul Keen (CEO) | **Scope**: the company, independent of any single go-to-market wedge | **Reviewed**: draft 2026-07-22

---

## What JetThoughts is

A Ruby on Rails engineering firm, shipping production software since 2011. Verifiable track record: 4.8/5 from 32 clients, ~95% client retention, five-year average client relationships, developers averaging 8+ years of experience. We work with founders, not enterprises.

## Mission

Help founders build and keep control of software they actually own and understand - and rescue them when someone else's build has taken that control away.

## Vision

Be the engineering partner a non-technical founder trusts with the truth about their software. Not the shop that talks over their head and holds the code hostage - the one that hands back a working product, the accounts, and a plain-English understanding of what they have.

## Positioning (the durable part)

**Trust and transparency is the product; the code is how we deliver it.** A founder's deepest pain is not a missing feature - it is not knowing whether they are being told the truth, and not owning what they paid for. JetThoughts sells that certainty: a senior engineer who reads the actual code, tells the founder straight, and hands back ownership at every step, with Paul on the call as the fractional-CTO translation layer.

This positioning is stable across whatever specific service we lead with. The **current expression** of it is the Vibe Code Rescue wedge (see the portfolio); the identity underneath - Rails engineering, ownership, radical transparency - does not change when the wedge does.

## Who we serve (ICP)

Non-technical founder, pre-seed to Series A, whose product was built by a dev shop, a freelancer, or an AI tool and who has lost confidence or control. Full profile: [`docs/90-99-content-strategy/strategy-analysis/90.10-icp-primary-website-target.md`](../90-99-content-strategy/strategy-analysis/90.10-icp-primary-website-target.md).

## What we will not be

- Not a body shop selling hours we can't tie to an outcome.
- Not a black box - if the founder can't understand what we did, we haven't finished.
- Not the cheapest option. We compete on trust and ownership, not price.
