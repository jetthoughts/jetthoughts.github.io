# Module 1 — Hypothesis & Smoke Test: Assessment Rubric

**Purpose**: Self-assessment rubric for M1 lessons. Matches quality gates from
30.02 ADR and 30.03 §3.4. Use after completing each lesson's success check.

---

## Per-Lesson Rubric

### Lesson 1.1: Form Your Founding Hypothesis

**Goal**: Write a one-sentence Founding Hypothesis you can falsify with strangers.

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Needs Work (2) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Customer specificity** | Names one role + industry ("solo chiropractors") | Names a role but vague industry ("small business owners") | "Small businesses" or "people who..." |
| **Problem clarity** | Uses customer's verbatim words from Reddit/G2/interview | Describes real problem in your own words | "Streamlining workflows" or market-research language |
| **Approach concreteness** | Names the mechanism ("one-click resubmit") | Names the outcome ("faster resubmission") | "AI-powered solution" or vague approach |
| **Differentiation** | Specific competitor named with why they'd switch | Names a competitor category ("billing services") | "Better" or "cheaper" without specifics |
| **Falsifiability** | A stranger can picture the product from the sentence alone | A stranger understands the problem but not the product | Nobody can tell what the product does |

**Gate**: Magic Lenses ≥14/20 (or ≥11/15 pre-revenue).

---

### Lesson 1.2a: Build the Page

**Goal**: Publish a smoke-test page that names your customer and their outcome.

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Needs Work (2) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Headline clarity** | Stranger names who it's for AND what it does in <3s | Stranger names one of two within 3s | Stranger can't name either |
| **Headline format** | Names specific person + outcome ("Solo chiropractors: resubmit denied claims in 30s") | Names outcome but vague customer | Generic headline ("Smart Solutions") |
| **CTA correctness** | "Get on the waitlist" + "Coming soon" disclaimer present | CTA present but no disclaimer | "Buy now" or CTA missing |
| **Extras stripped** | No Pricing, FAQ, Testimonials, or "How it works" blocks | One leftover block | Multiple leftover distraction blocks |

**Gate**: URL live + one stranger passes the 3-second clarity test.

---

### Lesson 1.2b: Wire Tracking

**Goal**: Install Microsoft Clarity and ad-platform pixel; verify with a session recording.

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Needs Work (2) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Clarity installed** | Own visit appears as session recording within 60s | Clarity installed but no recording yet | "No data yet" after 5+ min |
| **Pixel installed** | Ad-platform pixel in head-tag for correct channel (B2B vs B2C) | Pixel installed but wrong channel choice | No pixel installed |
| **Snippet placement** | Both in head-tag field (not body) | One in head, one misplaced | Both in body or custom-code without head/body distinction |

**Gate**: Own visit visible in Clarity recordings within 60 seconds.

---

### Lesson 1.2c: Run the Smoke Test

**Goal**: Buy cold traffic, read the conversion rate, make go/iterate/kill decision.

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Needs Work (2) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Channel selection** | Channel matches ICP (B2C→Meta, B2B→LinkedIn/Google) | Channel works but suboptimal for ICP | Channel picked "because I use it" |
| **Volume** | ≥300 visits before reading rate | 200-299 visits | <200 visits |
| **Test discipline** | No mid-test optimizations (headline, bid, audience unchanged) | One minor adjustment | Multiple mid-test changes |
| **Decision quality** | Decision matches the conversion band from the table | Decision correct but no second-channel verification for >20% | Ignores the conversion band ("feels right") |

**Gate**: ≥300 visits + conversion rate read against go/iterate/kill table.

---

### Lesson 1.3: Price Hypothesis

**Goal**: Add a Stripe price button and measure click-to-payment intent.

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Needs Work (2) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Stripe setup** | Payment link live, test mode verified, real mode ready | Payment link created but not verified | No payment link |
| **Price anchoring** | Price grounded in customer's current spend (what they pay today for the problem) | Price based on competitor research | Price pulled from "what feels right" |

**Gate**: ≥5% Stripe-click rate on the payment link.

---

## Module 1 Exit Rubric

**Milestone outcome**: Founding Hypothesis + live landing page with Stripe price button with conversion signal.

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Needs Work (2) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **All 5 lessons complete** | All success checks passed, all artifacts saved | 4 of 5 complete, one pending | 3 or fewer complete |
| **Hypothesis passes Magic Lenses** | ≥14/20 | 11-13/20 (pre-revenue acceptable) | <11/20 |
| **Smoke-test signal** | ≥6% email conversion OR ≥5% Stripe-click | 3-5% (iterate band — acceptable, will iterate in M2) | <3% (kill/pivot — restart M1) |
| **Bridge ready for M2** | Landing page URL + conversion data saved; hypothesis tightened per signal | Hypothesis not yet tightened | No hypothesis written |

**M1 exit gate**: Hypothesis passes Magic Lenses. Live landing page. Conversion signal read and decision made. Personal network outreach list started.
