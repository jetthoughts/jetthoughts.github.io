The Friday report, in plain English

Your last agency sent you a burndown chart and a Jira board you could not read. Every Friday one of our developers writes you this instead: one page, in the body of the email, no attachment.

Weekly report Friday, week 14
What shipped
Customers can pay with Apple Pay at checkout. Try it yourself on staging, sign in as demo@yourapp.test.
What's blocked
We need your Stripe live keys. Send them by Tuesday or the launch moves to the following week.
What's next
Order history screen. Two developers, four days. Marcos reviews it.

This shows the format. A real report names your staging URL, your pull requests, and the person who owes you an answer.
